Welcome to Cat Game!

Cat Game is a time-wasting turn-based game about cats!
And what's more fun than cats? Nothing, that's what.

The goal of the game is simple-- defeat the enemy before they defeat you! It's that easy.
Just keep clicking the "attack" button, and if your health reaches zero, you lose.
To be honest, there is no skill to this game; it is ALL luck.
